Chase Atlantic

Chase Atlantic is an Australian R&B band and production trio from Cairns, Queensland, formed in 2014. The group consists of three members: Christian Anthony, Clinton Cave and Mitchel Cave. Chase Atlantic has released seven EPs and three albums.

About

Guitarist, vocalist, saxophonist, and producer for the Australian alternative pop-rock band Chase Atlantic. The band's YouTube channel has earned over 2.2 million subscribers. They signed with Warner Brothers Records. In 2017, they released the hit song "Church."
